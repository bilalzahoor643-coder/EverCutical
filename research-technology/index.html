How We Work

Our Research Process

From cell sourcing to clinical delivery — every step meticulously controlled.

🧬

Step 01

Cell Sourcing

Premium cell lines derived from human umbilical cord mesenchymal stem cells (hUC-MSCs) and other specialized sources for maximum regenerative potential.

🔬

Step 02

Exosome Isolation

Proprietary purification technology isolates exosomes with maximum bioactivity and batch-to-batch consistency using advanced ultracentrifugation.

📋

Step 03

Quality Analysis

Multi-stage testing including nanoparticle tracking analysis, protein quantification, endotoxin screening, and sterility validation.

Step 04

Lyophilization

Advanced freeze-drying process preserves exosome integrity while enabling long-term storage and global distribution without degradation.

Step 05

Clinical Delivery

Final products packaged under GMP standards for professional clinical use in regenerative medicine and aesthetic dermatology.
