Everything You Need to Know About Exosome Technology

Explore answers to the most common questions about exosomes, regenerative science, clinical applications, and treatment expectations.

Trusted by

01

What Is an Exosome Treatment?

Exosome treatments utilize extracellular vesicles rich in growth factors, proteins, peptides, and signaling molecules to support cellular communication, tissue repair, and regenerative processes at the deepest cellular level.

02

How Do Exosomes Work?

Exosomes act as biological messengers, delivering molecular signals between cells to support regeneration, recovery, and cellular function. They carry bioactive cargo including growth factors, microRNA, and cytokines.

03

How Are Billions Counted in Exosomes?

The term "billions" refers to the concentration of exosome particles present in a formulation, indicating the number of biologically active vesicles available within a treatment volume.

04

Are Exosomes Safe?

High-quality exosome products are developed under strict laboratory and quality-control standards to ensure consistency, safety, and reliability. Every batch undergoes rigorous testing.

05

Exosome Therapy vs PRP: What Is the Difference?

Unlike PRP, exosomes provide highly concentrated signaling molecules without relying on donor variability, supporting more standardized regenerative outcomes.
